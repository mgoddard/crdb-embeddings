  ""Mexica"" is a board game designed by Wolfgang Kramer and Michael Kiesling and published in 2002 by Ravensburger in German and Rio Grande Games in English.  "Mexica" was awarded 5th prize in the 2002 Deutscher Spiele Preis. "Mexica" is the third game in the Mask Trilogy, after "Tikal (board game)|Tikal" and "Java (board game)|Java".
